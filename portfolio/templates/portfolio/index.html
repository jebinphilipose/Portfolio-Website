About Me

Hello, World!

I'm Jebin Philipose. I'm an undergraduate student studying towards a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science at Chitkara University, Punjab. Over the past 2 years, I have been developing my skills as a programmer. I believe in self-learning when it comes to learning anything, be it a new programming language or a framework. Therefore, I am comfortable in switching between multiple languages and learning a new thing isn't a problem. My interest lies in problem solving skills and development. I tried web development for a while and I really enjoy it. While making a website, I am responsible for its front-end as well as back-end. The websites which I make are responsive on all screen sizes. So basically, I am a self-made Full Stack Web Developer. I have a diverse set of skills, from HTML + CSS + Javascript, all the way to Python/Flask/Django and databases, to hosting the website on a linux server instance like GCE or Digital Ocean. I even graduated from Udacity's Full Stack Web Developer nanodegree. While I'm not programming, I enjoy spending time listening to music, playing football, watching movies and gaining knowledge about technical stuff.
